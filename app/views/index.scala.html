Wonder how to use application?

  • Find a ride

    All you need to do to find a Bikee you like is to use our Search! Easy and fast! You can search by entering the name of appropriate city or state. If you already know and like somebody from this place, you can also find him/her by entering his username.

  • Contact Renter

    Get your Bikee on time! After you have choosen a Bikee you would like to rent and date&time send request to Renter. Soon after sending the request you will be notified about your approval of renting.

  • Book a Bikee

    Bikee is ready for you! Last step before getting your Bikee is paying for your booking by credit card or Pay Pall. After that you can pick it up or ask Renter if he can deliver it to you.
